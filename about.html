About Standard Parti Poodles Australia

Passionate breeders dedicated to raising exceptional Standard Parti Poodles with love, care, and expertise.

Our Story

Welcome to Standard Parti Poodles Australia, where our passion for these magnificent dogs began over 13 years ago. Located in the picturesque town of Mansfield, Victoria, we have dedicated our lives to breeding exceptional Standard Parti Poodles that embody the perfect combination of beauty, intelligence, and temperament.

Our journey started with a love for the unique and striking parti coloration combined with the Standard Poodle's renowned intelligence and gentle nature. Over the years, we have built a reputation for producing healthy, well-socialized puppies that become cherished family members.

What sets us apart is our commitment to excellence in every aspect of our breeding program. From the initial selection of our breeding dogs to the early socialization of our puppies, every step is carefully planned and executed with love and dedication.

Our Breeding Philosophy

Quality over quantity - every decision we make is guided by what's best for our dogs and their future families.

Health First

All our breeding dogs undergo comprehensive health testing including hip and elbow scoring, eye clearances, and genetic testing for hereditary conditions. We believe healthy parents produce healthy puppies.

Temperament Matters

We carefully select our breeding dogs not only for their physical attributes but also for their excellent temperaments. Our dogs are calm, intelligent, and loving - qualities we pass on to their offspring.

Early Development

From birth, our puppies receive daily handling, early neurological stimulation, and exposure to various sounds, textures, and experiences to ensure they develop into confident, well-adjusted adults.

How Our Puppies Are Raised

Birth to 3 Weeks

Our newborn puppies are kept in a warm, quiet environment with their mother. We provide round-the-clock monitoring and begin early neurological stimulation exercises to promote optimal brain development.

3 to 6 Weeks

As the puppies become more mobile, we introduce them to various textures, sounds, and gentle handling. They begin interacting with their littermates and start the weaning process with high-quality puppy food.

6 to 8 Weeks

This crucial socialization period involves exposure to different people, children, household sounds, and basic grooming routines. Puppies receive their first health check and vaccinations from our veterinarian.

Ready for Home

At 8 weeks, our puppies are fully weaned, health checked, vaccinated, and ready to join their forever families. Each puppy comes with a comprehensive health guarantee and ongoing support.

Health Testing & Guarantees

Breeding Dog Health Tests

  • Hip Dysplasia: All breeding dogs have excellent or good hip scores
  • Elbow Dysplasia: Clear elbow scores for all breeding dogs
  • Eye Clearances: Annual eye examinations by certified ophthalmologists
  • Genetic Testing: DNA testing for hereditary conditions
  • General Health: Regular veterinary check-ups and health monitoring

Puppy Health Guarantee

Every puppy comes with:

  • Complete veterinary health examination
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations
  • Deworming protocol
  • 2-year genetic health guarantee
  • Microchip identification
  • Health certificate and records
